What are dogs?

Dogs are one of the two most common domestic animals worldwide, and they belong to the Canidae family (order Carnivora) of domestic mammals, according to Britannica (https://www.britannica.com/animal/dog). They has coexisted with humans for almost 12,000 years as hunting partners, defenders, subjects of admiration or derision, and friends.

Why dogs are good pets?

According to American Kennel Club (https://www.akc.org/expert-advice/lifestyle/10-science-based-benefits-dog/), raising a dog makes us feel less alone and good for our heart. Research shows that people with dogs have lower risks of death. More importantly, they encourage you to go outside and move. Also, dogs help us to social. People with dogs meet at parks and talk together, which make you more friends and better mental health.
